## 👨‍💻 About Me

Hi, I'm **Tanveer** a passionate **Full Stack Dev and MERN Stack** focused on building modern, scalable, and user-friendly web applications.

I Specifically work with **JavaScript & TypeScript**, turning ideas into real-world products using modern technologies. I enjoy working across both frontend and backend, creating seamless user experiences backed by efficient and reliable systems.

💡 I focus on writing **clean, maintainable, and scalable code**, not just making things work. I pay attention to performance, structure, and long-term maintainability.

🚀 I’m especially interested in building interactive and dynamic web experiences, including animations, modern UI/UX, and occasionally exploring 3D on the web.

📈 I strongly believe in **learning by building**, and I actively improve my skills by working on real projects, experimenting with new tools, and staying consistent with practice.

🤝 I'm always open to collaboration, feedback, and opportunities to grow as a developer.
